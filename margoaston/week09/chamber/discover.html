Discover Lakeside

Things to see and do in Lakeside:

The Lakes:

We have four lakes, El Capitan Lake, where you can fish, boat and jet ski. Lake Jennings has camping and fishing, San Vincente Reservoir has fishing and general boating, no water contact activities, and Lindo Lake has fishing and walking paths.

Golf:

For the golf enthusiast we have two golf courses. Barona Creek Golf Club and Willowbrook Golf Course.

Award Winning Library:

Our public library system has earned an award as being one of the top ten library systems in the nation.

Community Center:

Lunch is served daily to our senior friends. Individuals of all ages are invited to participate in a wide variety of classes and workshops.

The Lakeside Skatepark:

Show off your skills and practice your tricks at our family friendly skatepark.

Lakeside Rodeo Association:

We host a minimum of 2 sanctioned rodeos per year with numerous concerts and events.
